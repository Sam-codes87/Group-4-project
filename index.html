Water Intake

Screen Time

16. On a typical day, would you consider the total amount of time you spend looking at digital screens (phones, TVs, computers) healthy?

17. When you are trying to read a book, study, or complete a task, how often do you feel the urge to check your screens?

18. How frequently do you find yourself unexpectedly losing track of time while using screens for non-essential entertainment?

19. How dependent do you feel on your devices to feel entertained, relaxed, or mentally engaged?

20. To what extent do you feel that screen time interrupts your ability to be present or engage in real-life conversations?
